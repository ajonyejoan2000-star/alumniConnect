About Us

Built by Alumni, for Alumni

AlumniConnect is a vibrant digital community designed to empower, connect, and inspire Code Queen alumni through mentorship, networking, collaboration, and career opportunities.

The Code Queen initiative by the UK-registered charity Educating the Children is a highly impactful program tailored for young women and girls across East Africa, including Uganda. It provides free, intensive training in digital skills, coding, AI tools, and work readiness to bridge the gender gap in tech.

The platform brings together graduates, mentors, and industry professionals in one space where members can share experiences, build meaningful relationships, and grow together in tech and leadership.

Whether you're seeking guidance, professional connections, events, or a supportive community — AlumniConnect is built to help every member learn, lead, and thrive.
